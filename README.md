# 🎓 NBKR Institute of Science and Technology Website

Welcome to the official website project of NBKR Institute of Science and Technology (NBKRIST), located in Vidyanagar, Tirupati District.
This website is designed to provide a comprehensive overview of the institution, its academic offerings, infrastructure, and student experience.

---

## 🌐 Project Overview

This website serves as a digital platform for students, parents, and visitors to explore the college. It presents essential information in a structured and user-friendly manner, covering academics, facilities, certifications, and contact details.

---

## 🏠 Home Page

-The Home page acts as the main gateway and includes:

**Courses We Offer**
-A brief introduction to the academic programs available at the institution.

**Our Global Campus**
-Highlights of the campus environment and its academic reach.

**Our Facilities**
-Overview of infrastructure such as classrooms, labs, libraries, and other resources.

**What Our Students Say**
-Testimonials reflecting student experiences and satisfaction.

---

## 📖 About Page

-The About page provides detailed information about the institution:

**About Us**
-Covers the history, vision, mission, and core values of NBKRIST, emphasizing its commitment to quality education and innovation.

## 🎓 Courses Page

-The Courses page gives a deeper insight into academics:

**Courses We Offer**
-Detailed list of undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

**Facilities**
-Information about academic support systems such as laboratories, libraries, and modern learning tools.

---

## 📰 Blog Page

-The Blog page focuses on updates and opportunities:

**Our Certifications**
